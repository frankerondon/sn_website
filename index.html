About us


SN Integrity Pty Ltd was established in 2017 in Australia to provide smart solutions for asset integrity. Our vision is “to become the preferred provider of state of the art ultrasonic technologies”. We have partnered with worldwide industry-leading firms in the ultrasonic field, with over 30 years of combined experience in inspection and corrosion in oil and gas facilities onshore / offshore, enabling us to support you with cost-effective selection and application of high quality products to manage your asset’s integrity.
